Professional Caregiving Workshop Module 2
MISSION

To consistently deliver quality care for an individual, organization and community.

VISION

To equip the caregiver with the right attitude, skill and knowledge for better in patient or family outcomes.

VALUES

Professionalism
Team
Learning Culture
Respect
Humility

INTRODUCTION

Professional Caregiver Workshop is a planned process whereby all carer are introduced and familiarized with their main role in patient care. This includes collaborating with their co-workers and being focus in the patient outcome. In addition, this training include such basic topics related to the procedures in Basic Activities of Daily Living.

OBJECTIVES

At the end of the workshop, participants will be able to:

1. State their main roles and responsibilities in assisting individual /patient care.

2. Enhance their knowledge in assisting activity daily living of an individual /patient.

3. Demonstrate effective skills in assisting patient care includes basic activity daily living.

4. Practice a positive attitude towards individual's/ patient's needs.

5. Build up self-professionalism, knowledge, skills and morale partly as a caregiver/worker.

6. Enhance effective communication technique in Malay languages to provide knowledge, attitude, and skills in communication with patients and family.
